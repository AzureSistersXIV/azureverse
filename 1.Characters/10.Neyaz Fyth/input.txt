Few things are known about Neyaz Fyth in Golmore Jungle. Calm and thoughful woman, she led a peaceful life there, with a few friends who liked her. That's why her desire to leave the Jungle and the following banishment were a surprise who knew her then. She let a trail behind her of people stunned by her beauty and her wise mind.

That's a pure random moment who made her meet L'envie D'aimer in an inn in Limsa Lominsa, right after arriving in Eorzea. The thoughtful woman discussed a long time with a smart and skillful woman in need of help with the many needs of her work and life. Nobody, apart from herself, knows why but she decided there to serve L'envie, to the surprise of the au'ra herself. Neyaz told L'envie at that moment she saw grandeur and a legend in the making in L'envie. The au'ra took a few seconds before accepting Neyaz as her retainer (even if Neyaz jokes she is a humble servant to her mistress).

Since that moment, she can be found in one of L'envie houses, working there as her retainer. In her rest moments, she likes to daw tarot with the cards she uses as an astromancer. Eating peaches and mirabelle plums at the same time, she can uses the cards to read your future if you bring some to her.
