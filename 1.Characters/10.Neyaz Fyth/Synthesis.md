**Character Synthesis: Neyaz Fyth**  
*"The Celestial Guide Between Whimsy and Wisdom"*  

---

### **Overview**  
Neyaz Fyth, a Rava Viera whose centuries-old soul dances between the whimsical and the profound, is a paradox of starlit sagacity and playful mischief. As an astromancer-healer and devoted retainer, she weaves fate with the flick of a tarot card, guiding her charge, L’envie D’aimer, toward an unwritten legend. Her presence—a blend of ethereal grace and cheeky charm—leaves trails of curiosity and warmth, masking the enigmatic past that led her from the Golmore Jungle’s shadows to Eorzea’s bustling heart.  

---

### **Appearance: The Verdant Oracle**  
Neyaz’s visage is a living tapestry of the forest and cosmos, merging Viera elegance with astral mystique:  
- **Hair & Eyes:** Dark green hair cascades in twin pigtails, adorned with fresh flowers that shift with the seasons. Her bangs frame luminous dark green eyes, deep as ancient groves.  
- **Ears & Marks:** Long, velvety rabbit ears twitch with keen awareness, often crowned with blossoms. A delicate green tattoo curves across her nose, mirroring constellations, while dark green lipstick hints at secrets untold.  
- **Aura:** Her posture exudes serenity, but a mischievous glint in her gaze betrays her love for gentle chaos.  

---

### **Background: From Grove to Guiding Star**  

#### **Echoes of Golmore**  
Born in the Golmore Jungle, Neyaz lived as a quiet guardian of its secrets, revered for her wisdom and healing prowess. Her sudden departure—and subsequent banishment—shocked her kin. Whispers linger: did she defy tribal edicts, or did the stars themselves compel her exile? She left behind admirers haunted by her beauty and cryptic farewells, her absence a riddle wrapped in vines.  

#### **Fated Encounter**  
In a Limsa Lominsa inn, destiny intervened. Neyaz crossed paths with **L’envie D’aimer**, an ambitious Au Ra whose restless spirit mirrored her own. Over shared wine and starlight, Neyaz glimpsed L’envie’s latent potential—*“The stars led me to you to see you grow as the leader fate stipulated you would be.”* Against all logic, the centuries-old Viera pledged herself as the Au Ra’s retainer, trading jungle solitude for the chaos of Eorzean intrigue.  

#### **Astromancer in Service**  
Now, Neyaz balances duty and delight. By day, she manages L’envie’s estates with preternatural efficiency; by night, she deciphers tarot spreads under the moon. Her price for a reading? A basket of fruits—strawberries preferred, though peaches and plums suffice. Her accuracy is uncanny, her advice veiled in metaphor: *“A thorned rose blooms brightest after rain… and you, my dear, are overdue for a storm.”*  

---

### **Personality: The Twilight Jester**  
- **The Sage:** Neyaz’s calm demeanor and astrological insights make her a trusted confidante. She heals wounds—physical and emotional—with herbal poultices and cryptic comfort.  
- **The Trickster:** Ever-playful, she engineers “compromising situations” to keep L’envie humble—a misplaced robe here, a strategically timed tarot revelation there.  
- **The Seeker:** Beneath her mirth lies a hunger for cosmic truth. She views L’envie’s rise as a grand experiment: can mortal ambition align with celestial design?  

---

### **Relationships: Threads of Fate**  
- **L’envie D’aimer:** Her “mistress” and muse. Neyaz’s loyalty is absolute, but her methods are unorthodox—she’ll nudge L’envie into scandals to teach resilience.  
- **Golmore’s Ghosts:** She never speaks of her past, though her songs sometimes echo jungle lullabies. Those who inquire receive a strawberry and a wink.  
- **Fruit Vendors:** Her obsession with strawberries has made her a legend in markets. Farmers save their sweetest harvests for her, hoping to earn a free reading.  

---

### **Legacy: The Unwritten Arcana**  
Neyaz’s true age remains a mystery (she claims “one hundred and half…ish”), but her impact is timeless. To L’envie, she is both anchor and compass; to Eorzea, a enigma wrapped in petals. Some say she’s a fallen star; others, a goddess testing mortals. Neyaz laughs at all theories—*“Why choose one truth when the universe offers so many?”* Her endgame? To forge L’envie into a legend… and perhaps, in doing so, rewrite her own fate.  

---

### **Trivia Echoes**  
- **Fruitful Divination:** Her tarot deck is stained with strawberry juice—a quirk clients find endearing or unnerving.  
- **Eternal Spring:** The flowers in her hair never wilt. “A little astromancy,” she shrugs, though botanists suspect primal magic.  
- **Signature Tactic:** She “accidentally” leaves love letters addressed to L’envie in public places, chuckling as her flustered boss deflects suitors.  

*“The stars guide, but never command. And I? I simply… nudge.”*
