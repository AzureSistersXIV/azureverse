Claire is strong enigma to many observers. Calm, strict and undoubtly caustic, she tends to serve L'envie D'aimer in all aspects of her life, be them personal or professionnal. It can be observed she is greatly professional to L'envie needs, answering fast to each of her commands.

Strange thing, because Claire is known for coming from Kyōju-no-Sato, a village presumed to be under the Sapphire Sea in the far East. Her first appearance in Kugane with a bundle on her shoulder and a paper in her hand. She traveled long and far, defending herself as a ninja against all bandits trying to steal or brutalize her. Observant, workaholic and focused, she traveled until she found L'envie D'aimer in Gridania while she was doing some run in local merchants. The meeting was more than formal in the eyes of the people present at the time. Nothing explain then why she follow L'envie like her shadows since their meeting.

Claire is an avid reader and great appreciator of the arts. She keeps to herself most of the time but the arrival of someone brutish and galivanting may open that cold exterior.
