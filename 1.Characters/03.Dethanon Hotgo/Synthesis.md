**Character Synthesis: Dethanon Hotgo**  
*"A Star-Crossed Guardian Between Blade and Love"*  

---

### **Overview**  
Dethanon Hotgo, a Xaela Au Ra mercenary with a soul as tempestuous as the steppes of his youth, embodies the duality of a hardened warrior and a tender protector. His life—a tapestry of celestial wanderlust, violent reckonings, and redemptive love—culminates in a legacy that transcends bloodshed, etched into the hearts of those he cherished.  

---

### **Appearance: The Storm’s Visage**  
Dethanon’s imposing frame is marked by the stark contrasts of his heritage:  
- **Hair & Eyes:** Very short, dark blue hair mirrors the night sky, paired with piercing dark blue eyes that gleam like distant stars.  
- **Scales & Marks:** Black scales cascade across his nose, jaw, arms, and legs, resembling armor. Red mascara streaks the outer corners of his eyes, evoking both war paint and silent sorrow.  
- **Horns & Tail:** Jet-black horns curve like blades, and his serpentine tail flicks with restless energy, symbols of the “inner beast” he wrestled to control.  
- **Physique:** Lithe yet muscular, his body bears scars from countless battles, including the cryptic injuries on his torso—a map of his fractured past.  

---

### **Background: From Steppe to Sea**  

#### **Childhood Under the Stars**  
Born into the nomadic Hotgo tribe of the Azim Steppe, young Dethanon was a dreamer who found solace in the constellations. The stars whispered promises of worlds beyond the horizon, compelling him to abandon tribal life for a mercenary’s path. His Xaela upbringing instilled ferocity, but his heart yearned for discovery.  

#### **Mercenary Ascension**  
In Hingashi, Dethanon honed his skills as a sellsword, embracing the region’s culture—learning the **shamisen**, savoring its cuisine, and adopting its customs. His “inner beast,” a primal force nurtured by tribal combat, made him a feared adversary. He rose through mercenary ranks, joining a notorious band, but this brotherhood soon corroded his spirit.  

#### **Fall and Isolation**  
After five shadowy years, Dethanon emerged broken in a Kugane slum—battered, alcoholic, and haunted. He retreated into solitude, taking lone missions to survive. His reputation dwindled, but his resolve hardened.  

#### **Redemption in Kyofu-no-Sato**  
A delivery mission to the submarine village **Kyofu-no-Sato** altered his fate. Enchanted by its bioluminescent landscapes and warm villagers, he forged bonds with elders, children, and eventually, **Jasmine Istana**, the future “Princess of Azure Currents.” Their love defied tradition, birthing a daughter, **Désirée**. Fatherhood softened Dethanon; he traded blades for diplomacy, his laughter (a rarity) even coaxing joy from the stoic Jasmine—a feat none repeated.  

#### **Tragic End**  
When former mercenary comrades threatened his family, Dethanon’s dormant beast resurged. He fought fiercely to protect Kyofu-no-Sato, but perished in the clash. His final words to Désirée—*“Don’t pity me, my child. Go forward. And fly, angel, fly as far your wings let you go”*—became her compass.  

---

### **Personality: The Dichotomy**  
- **The Warrior:** Pragmatic, territorial, and ruthlessly efficient in combat. His “beast” manifests in a fighting style blending Xaela ferocity with Hingan precision.  
- **The Dreamer:** A poet at heart, he marveled at foreign cultures, collected stories, and stargazed with Désirée.  
- **The Protector:** Post-Jasmine, his cynicism melted into selflessness. He traded ale for lullabies, fists for patience, though guilt over past violence lingered.  

---

### **Relationships: Anchors and Tempests**  
- **Jasmine Istana:** Their love was a quiet rebellion. Jasmine, initially drawn to his mystique, found in him a partner who revered her strength, not just her title.  
- **Désirée Hotgo:** His “angel,” whose curiosity mirrored his own. He often took her to Kyofu-no-Sato, teaching her of the stars.  
- **The Mercenary Band:** A brotherhood turned betrayal. Their return forced Dethanon to confront the man he once was—and reject him.  

---

### **Legacy: The Unwritten Stars**  
Dethanon’s tale is whispered in Kyofu-no-Sato’s tides and Hingashi’s taverns. To some, he is a cautionary tale of a beast consumed by its rage; to others, a martyr who chose love over glory. His greatest legacy, Désirée, carries his wanderlust, her wings unbound by his sacrifice.  

*“The stars he loved now guide her. And in their light, his soul soars.”*
