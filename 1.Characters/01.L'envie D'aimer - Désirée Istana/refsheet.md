About L'envie D'aimer
Born Désirée, she is the daughter of the Princess of the Azure Currents, Jasmine Istana. Growing up under the care of her father Dethanon, she is a happy child and very sociable with all adults and children.

Following the dramatic events leading to the murder of her father before her own eyes, and her aetherical reaction leading to the deaths of both murderers, Désirée is quarantined to learn how to control her powerful ether. This led to social isolation where she lost contact with everyone she knows. After a few years, other lessons emerged. Etiquette lessons and others, everything about her future status as Princess of the Azure Currents.

At the age of fourteen, she reappears in her village, a depressed and lonely teenager. She tried to start relationships again, but the lost time created rumors that she was a monster in waiting, a powerful demon that the current Princess raised to scare them all and become a demon princess.

After a while, she managed to help the elderly and they accepted her again. She learned from them how to contact her fellow teenagers but the rumors remained too alive for them to let her approach them. These rumors grew worse as many people became suspicious of the daughter and mother. People were complaining more and more about the current Princess & her politics. A group of men began to move around. And some elders alerted Désirée that a coup was possible.

After learning some ether manipulation from an elder, Désirée went to her mother, sensing the people's anger and asking her mother to accompany her, flee the region and start again elsewhere. The mother dismissed her own daughter and told her to assume her position. Désirée decided that her life was more important than the duties they were going to lose. The mother then exiled her daughter, calling her a nobody, removing her name from the line of succession. Désirée took the news well and changes her name to L'envie D’aimer. It was the beginning of a life full of downs, leading to personal hell. And many ups, which lead her to a better life.
