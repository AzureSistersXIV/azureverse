### **Extensive Character Description: L'envie D’aimer**  
*From Final Fantasy XIV (Original Character)*  

---

### **Background**  
- **Birth Name**: Désirée Istana.  
- **Race/Gender**: Raen Au Ra (Female).  
- **Origins**:  
  - Daughter of Princess Jasmine Istana (Raen royalty of Kyōju-no-Saton underwater village under the Sapphire Sea) and Dethanon Hotgo (Xaela Au Ra of the Hotgo tribe, Azim Steppe).  
  - Witnessed her father’s murder at age 7, triggering an uncontrollable aetherial outburst that killed his attackers. Quarantined for years to master her volatile aether, she endured isolation and rumors of being a "demon princess."  
  - Exiled by her mother at 16 for searching to flee the upcoming coup d'etat against her family with her mother, she renamed herself **L'envie D’aimer** ("The Desire to Love") and fled to the sea surface.  
- **Trauma**: Captured by pirates, sexually abused for months, and rescued by the mercenary group **G.A.S.T.** (Gyr Abania Strike Team). Developed a deep fear of men and leaned into her lesbian identity.  
- **Rebirth**: After leaving G.A.S.T., she founded **Azure Treasures Co.**, a lucrative import/export business, with a successfull crafting side business.  

---

### **Appearance**  
- **Hair**: Long, dark blue with a lighter blue streak in her front bangs (princess cut).  
- **Eyes**: Heterochromatic—light blue (left) and dark blue (right).  
- **Scales**: White scales adorn her forehead, nose, jaws, arms, legs, and feet.  
- **Unique Features**: Ivory horns (replacing ears) and tail.  
- **Attire**: Prefers practical blue outfits reminding her of her origins, yet she can wears carefully sewed dresses and robes if needed.  

---

### **Personality**  
- **Resilient**: Forged by trauma, she rebuilds her life repeatedly, turning pain into ambition.  
- **Protective**: Collects "stray" women as retainers and allies, offering them help through work and services.  
- **Duality**: Struggles to balance destructive aether (black magic) with healing (white magic), mirroring her internal conflict between rage and compassion.  
- **Entrepreneurial**: A shrewd businesswoman who trades in rare artifacts and crafts, but wrestles with moral compromises.  
- **Vulnerable**: Haunted by self-doubt and fear of losing control, especially around Minfilia Warde.  

---

### **Abilities**  
- **Aetherial Mastery**:  
  - Wields dual magic (black and white) simultaneously, a rare feat.  
  - **Mutation**: Exposure to particular women’s pheromones triggers a phallus transformation (a lineage trait from her tribe), forcing her to reconcile primal urges with self-control.  
- **Combat**: Skilled in fire magic (former black mage) and healing. Prefers strategic, non-lethal resolutions.  
- **Crafting**: Mastery in carpentry, blacksmithing, sewing, tannery, alchemy, and culinary arts.  

---

### **Key Relationships**  
1. **Sex-friends**:  
   - **Minfilia Warde**: Minfilia’s scent triggers L'envie’s aetherial mutation, leading to a complex bond blending dominance, healing, and vulnerability.  
   - **Y’shtola**: Invited by Minfiia into their open relationship, Y’shtola half studies, and the other half enjoys, L’envie’s peculiarity, turning their relationship between one of curiosity and sexual satisfaction.
   - **Ceres Arcadia**: Ceres’s scent triggers L'envie’s aetherial mutation and profit of it, turning their relationship into one of sexual teasing, emotional support and mutual satisfaction into oblivion.

2. **Retainers**:  
   - **Neyaz Fyth (Viera)**: Exceptional witty and mindful adviser to most L'envie ventures since their meeting.
   - **Galare Nouise (Hyur)**: Unlucky but joyous servant, trustful secrets keeper.
   - **Claire Dejais (Xaela)**: Stoic ninja bodyguard with a dry wit, keeping ahead of her to assure all her wishes are ready when asked.  
   - **Fureur Devaincre (Hyur)**: Ex-thief turned sharpshooter and information gatherer, looking for them from forgotten and overlooked people.

3. **Family**:  
   - **Orghana Hotgo**: Her Xaela grandmother, last surviving elder of the Hotgo tribe.  
   - **Fanny/Plaisir D’aimer**: Her twin sister, raised in the Steppe after being sent away to save her from abandonment or death.  

---

### **Role in the Story**  
- **Mercenary Years**: Fought with G.A.S.T. during the Seventh Umbral Calamity, surviving Carteneau and saving lives through long forgotten white magic.  
- **Post-Calamity**: Clashing with G.A.S.T. leader Fhurai over moral compromises, founded Azure Treasures Co., create and retrieve artefacts for rich patrons.  
- **Scions Alliance**: Provides intel about rare artefacts, help retrieve them with retainers help.  
- **Azim Steppe Quest**: Discovers her Xaela roots and reunites with her twin sister, resolving lifelong identity struggles.  

---

### **Themes & Conflicts**  
- **Control vs. Chaos**: Battles her aether’s destructive impulses while striving to heal others.  
- **Identity**: Rejects royal duty and "demon princess" rumors to forge a legacy of empowerment.  
- **Morality**: Balances shady contracts with ethical craftsmanship, aiding beast tribes despite Eorzean prejudice.  
- **Trauma & Healing**: Uses her own business to create opportunities for women to better themselves and their lives.  

---

### **Legacy**  
- **Symbol of Resilience**: From abused exile to trade magnate, she proves shattered souls can rebuild empires.  
- **Diplomat**: Bridges Eorzea and beast tribes using her mercenary ties and artifact trade.  
- **Mentor**: Inspires retainers and allies to embrace their strength, as seen in her cabaret’s role as a mean to work.  

---
