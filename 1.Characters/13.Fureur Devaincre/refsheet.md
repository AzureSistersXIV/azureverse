About Fureur Devaincre
A young ishgardian delinquant without name, the young woman called Fureur didn't have a promising future for herself in her childhood. Living from petty thefts and scraps, she survived by the strengh of her self-denial in front of death. Surviving the end of the Dragonsong war in the slume of the Brume, she hoped the opportunities at the end of the war would help her but none appeared to her.

With a gun in her hand and her intelligence, she decided to provock luck by shooting a few monsters in Ishgard proximity and continuing her petty thefts. Bad luck for as she tried to steal from L'envie D'aimer party when they visited Ishgard for one of their first times. Bad luck as she was immediatly catched by L'envie's retainer Claire who put her in the Temple Knights hands. Tried quickly and ready to be put in prison for her misdeeds, her luck finally turned as L'envie propose to allievate her jail time into a retainer service time, as her own retainer.

Washed, fed, housed with a job of her own and a name given to her by her new mistress, Fureur had then a new life opening in her path. Since that moment, she follows her new mistress, living a life of wild, exciting adventures.
