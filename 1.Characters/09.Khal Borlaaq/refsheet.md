Important Notes
Khal is a particular individual. Born in the Borlaaq tribe as a male, he was rejected from the tribe and grew up in the Iriq tribe. As much he grew, he felt as he couldn't fit in tribe who welcomed him. Once adult, he decided to live by himself.

The job of adventurer wasn't an obvious one to him but the wonders of Othard as a whole, followed right after by the sights of Hingashi led him to be awestruck and in love with the different cultures who surrounded him at that exact moment. Who made him spend a few years in Kugane, doing many jobs with a good income. Once the life in Kugane became too dull to his taste, he tried his luck to this weird continent called Eorzea.

Bad luck for him as he arrived a few months before a new threat from beastmen in Eorzea. Wanting to help, he joined the numbers of adventurers helping the city-states. That's how he met the Gyr Abania Strike Team (G.A.S.T.) free company. In the inn where they spoke together, he heard their plea for Ala Mhigo et became sympathetic to their cause. He joined them, bringing mirth, excitement and laughs. Hiding a thoughtful and philosophical side.
