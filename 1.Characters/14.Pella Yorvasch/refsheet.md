About Pella Yorvasch
Pella is a strange woman for the eorzean observer. Brought back from Tural in L'envie D'aimer party, her story went through the rumor mill. What is sure is L'envie D'aimer, her sister and the rest of the party found her by a lake in Yak Tel, in a unconscious state. L'envie personally healed her with her retainer Neyaz help.

Other assured detail is Pella being a viper, chasing powerful creatures to kill them if they are a danger to natural order. Her knowledge and aptitudes gives her an edge than even Claire cannot overcome as a ninja.

Nothing else is known at the moment about her. But surely this page will be updated when more information will be more common about her.
