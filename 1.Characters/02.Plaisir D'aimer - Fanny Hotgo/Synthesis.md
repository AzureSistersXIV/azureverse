### Character Profile Synthesis: Plaisir D'aimer  
*"Play smart and people will notice you. Play dumb and most people will dismiss you. Wonder what I do?"*  

---  

#### **Core Identity**  

- **Name**: Plaisir D’aimer (born Fanny Hotgo)  
- **Race/Gender**: Raen Au Ra (Female)  
- **Role**: Bodyguard, Monster Hunter, and nomadic warrior of the Azim Steppe.  
- **Sexuality**: Bisexual, with relationships forged through rivalry and mutual respect.  
- **Key Traits**: Pragmatic, fiercely resilient, distrustful of strangers, protective of loved ones, and driven to defy prejudice through sheer skill.  

---  

#### **Appearance**  

- **Hair**: Short dark blue bob cut with a lighter blue patch in the front bangs.  
- **Scales**:  
  - White scales on forehead, nose, jaws, arms, forearms, hands, hips, legs, and feet.  
  - Black scales on her temples, mimicking horns as a tribute to her Xaela father.  
- **Eyes**: Heterochromatic—left eye dark blue, right eye light blue.  
- **Unique Features**: Ivory-white horns (replacing ears) and a matching tail.  

---  

#### **Backstory Highlights**  

1. **Exile & Survival**:  
   - Born Fanny to a Xaela father (Dethanon) and Raen mother, she was exiled alongside her grandmother Orghana by the Hotgo tribe for her Raen heritage, who seems to make her weaker than the average xaela.  
   - Raised in harsh conditions, she learned to hunt and fight to protect Orghana, whom she affectionately calls "the old crone."  

2. **Warrior’s Path**:  
   - Faced relentless discrimination as the only Raen in the Steppe. Turned combat into a survival tool, defeating beasts and warriors alike.  
   - Developed a reputation for brash trashtalking, developing a simple minded persona to outwit her enemies—a skill that earned begrudging respect from Xaela tribes.  

3. **Rivalries & Recognition**:  
   - Rose to prominence by challenging Khans and Khatuns, matching even Oronir and Dotharl warriors in combat.  
   - Engages in weekly deathless duels with Sadu Dotharl, which evolved into a power struggle for dominance—and mutual admiration.  

4. **Bonds Beyond Battle**:  
   - Formed an unexpected friendship with Cirina Mol, who helped her see value in trust and connection despite her ingrained wariness.  
   - Struggles with an inner "locked" power, unable to unleash her primal rage like her foes, hinting at untapped potential.  

5. **Awaiting Change**:  
   - Stares at the stars during sleepless nights, sensing an impending shift tied to **upcoming important character**—a force poised to redefine her world.  

---  

#### **Motivations & Relationships**  

- **Drives**: Protect Orghana at all costs, prove her worth in a prejudiced society, and uncover the source of her restrained power.  
- **Found Family**:  
  - **Orghana**: Her grandmother and sole anchor. Their bond is rooted in survival, yet softened by dark humor and shared defiance.  
  - **Sadu Dotharl**: A fiery rival whose clashes blend combat with unspoken camaraderie. Their fights now dictate leadership in more than battle.  
  - **Cirina Mol**: A friend who challenges her isolation, teaching her that trust can be as vital as strength.  

---  

#### **Themes & Conflicts**  

- **Survival vs. Belonging**: Balances her instinct to distrust strangers with a growing desire for acceptance.  
- **Strength Through Restraint**: Her inability to unleash raw power forces her to rely on strategy, making her a uniquely disciplined warrior.  
- **Legacy of Exile**: Wrestles with her Raen identity in a Xaela-dominated world, determined to carve a legacy that transcends her father’s choices.  

---  

#### **Quote to Live By**  

*"I fight only to survive and see tomorrow, not for glory or dominance. I don't care about that."*  

Plaisir D’aimer embodies defiance in the face of exclusion, turning every scar and slight into fuel for her unyielding resolve. Her story is one of survival evolving into purpose—a testament to the power of grit over fate.
