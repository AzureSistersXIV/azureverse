About Galare Nouise
Galare Nouise is a midlander woman born in Thanalan, from unknown parents. If she is brave from a young age, this is to defend herself from the bad luck she seemes to have inherited from her family. She became known at Ul'dah years later as a pretty woman helping merchants with their merchandise. Helpful as possible, she fight at every moment her unluck and get better in life. Making people desire to help her.

Gullible, unlucky and friendly enough (her bad luck making lots of people avoid her), she has some bad times where she has to accept odd jobs to survive. What made nearly sign her life in servitude to Ul’dah slavers. By a huge stroke of luck, one L'envie D'aimer passed the two parties at the right moment and helped Galare to avoid an ungrateful life. Thanking all gods and powers putting L'envie in her midst, Galare decided to repay her debt by serving the blue-haired woman as her retainer. Well, as much her rescinding bad luck permits her.
