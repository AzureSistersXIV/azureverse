# The Steppe of my father  

If the Ruby Sea is a strange reminder of her birthplace with all the places under the water, [L'envie D'aimer](https://characterhub.com/character/lenvie-daimer?v=1) is amazed when she passes the cavern and enters fully into the [Azim Steppe](https://arealmremapped.com/Othard/The_Azim_Steppe/The_Azim_Steppe.html). The sun shines, the green fields stretch as far as the eye can see. In the landscape, the big platform of Azim Khaat stands as an eye-catcher. She looks at the reliefs all around her and marvels at everything she sees.  

“No wonder we couldn’t find anything about your family here… Look at that place!”  

[Fureur](https://characterhub.com/character/fureur-devaincre?v=1)’s exclamation brings L'envie out of her contemplation. Every member of her retinue is as awestruck as she was moments ago. The Steppe is a wonder to everyone, even Claire, who clearly shows surprise at what she sees. L'envie smiles at the sight—this is not something she witnesses every day. But they have a goal to pursue now. She must play the leader again.  

“Ladies, please. We have someone to find, and our contacts, in their cryptic ways, gave us a path to follow. Let’s go to Reunion.”  

While [Neyaz](https://characterhub.com/character/neyaz-fyth?v=1) and Galare quickly nod in acceptance of her orders, Claire has already gone ahead to scout, and Fureur responds with her usual energy. Picking up her things, L'envie sets off toward the location surrounded by stones, which presents itself at the very end of the path. The activity there matches that of a bustling village, far livelier than the Kyofu-no-Sato of her childhood. Her eyes are now accustomed to crowds, but it doesn’t dampen her eagerness to explore the market. Watching her retainers stamp their feet impatiently, she smiles at them before uttering a welcome sentence.  

“Go, we can do some shopping. But remember, we have a long road ahead—save the big purchases for the way home.”  

The smiles she receives in return tell her she’s made her friends happy. Following them, she strolls through the market, eyeing the merchandise. Meats of all kinds and cuts, even unfamiliar ones, fill the stalls. Hides are sold for rugs and clothes. What truly catches her attention is the handcrafted jewelry made from fabrics, leather, and trinkets. The designs intrigue her, and the near-empty stand suggests they sell well. She smiles at the Xaela merchant, who returns a wary grin. Perhaps the woman isn’t used to Raen strangers. L'envie continues her stroll and buys a fruit at the next stall. There, too, the merchant wears an uneasy expression. Maybe his face mirrors her own. Ten years later, she still reels from her trauma. Her psychiatrist will revisit this soon. Shaking it off, she sits at the end of the main alley, watching people while finishing her fruit. The Steppe’s inhabitants live simply, and L'envie can’t stop imagining this could have been her life had her father returned to his tribe with her.  

After finishing her fruit, L'envie gets back on her feet. She returns to the market and begins questioning people about the Hotgo tribe. The news is not what she expects. The tribe was recently massacred by the Dotharl. The only survivors are those who left to travel alone and weren’t present during the attack. L'envie’s mood darkens. Has her only lead to her origins vanished the moment she arrived? She asks if anyone knows more, but no one answers. Defeated, she returns to Reunion’s entrance and waits. Was she too hopeful to imagine finding family here? Her thoughts churn until a throat-clearing interrupts her. She turns to meet Claire’s dead-serious gaze.  

“Ma’am. I have information you may find interesting.”  
“Yes, Claire. I do too. But let’s wait for everyone to return. Please.”  

Claire’s face softens with compassion—she must have uncovered the same grim news. Neyaz and Galare return together, their dynamic unchanged: Galare needs grounding from her bad luck, and Neyaz needs reminders to stay present. Fureur arrives last, her energy undimmed. Though younger, her vivacity makes L'envie feel decades older. The blue-haired woman turns to Claire.  

“So, your information, please?”  
“The Hotgo tribe is no more. Decimated by the Dotharl. They wore vibrant face paints and dyed their hair blue with their moods.”  
“Legends say their tribe birthed twins from an iron giant—a boy and girl with matching hair,” adds Neyaz.  
“No clear reason for the Dotharl’s attack,” says Galare. “Some say absorption is common here. Maybe the Hotgo refused?”  
“Dotharl believe in cyclical rebirth,” Neyaz counters. “Old souls return as new babies with new names and faces.”  

Footsteps approach the women and they watch a group of xaela men in green. They look dressed to impress with clothes fitting their masculine forms. Their smiles towards L'envie and her retinue sense a sense of unease as they approach. As if their presence together screams danger. Désirée's cane is on the ground with the rest of her belongings. If the men decided to attack, she would have to take matters into her own hands, personally.

“Look, a raen !”
“She is well accompanied, at least.”
“Do you think we can take one or two to the Dawn throne ?”
“I would gladly take care of the raen and put her in her place, dead at our feet.”
“The bunny girl, I want the bunny girl.”
“The brown gal look feisty, I want to break her !”

Before any move can be done by any of the group, a member of the Qestir tribe came at their contact before using the sign language to tell people to fight outside Reunion. Neyaz understood the language and permit people to everyone to understand what the Qestir tell. L'envie takes her stuff and wait for the men to leave. Knowing they are scheming a ambush, L'envie and her retinue prepared themselves and waited a bit. Once a feet outside Reunion, L'envie roll to avoid an arrow who targeted her. Neyaz put a shield around them all before Fureur took a shot, catching a second arrow with a bullet. Galare put herself in the front, shield up and ready to intercept. Two of the men jump to them, great axes ready to fight. Claire appear from the shadows to attack the ranged attackers while L'envie heal everyone, putting regeneration effets on everyone in case of problems.  Claire attain fast the archers and make them back down. Fureur target and disable one, giving Claire the surprise effect on the second one. Neyaz slow the two warrior in their contact. Galare, with a upwards thrust of her sword, throw one of the warrior towards the sky while L'envie combine her two talents with a ball of white magic wind in a hand and a ball of black magic ice in the other to create an ice storm who throw away the second warrior.

“What the hell is that ?”
“Did she used two different magicks at the same time !?”
“Get back ! We need to inform the others a raen monster in on the plain !”

The four men ran away like rabbits. L'envie watched them, no impressed by their performance. They really wanted to kill her ? She has more difficult battles with Garlemald. Small little boys, all four of them. Sighing, she thought of their discussion. Her one lead, gone in smoke.

“In the end, we have no real reason to stay here now. The Hotgo seems gone.”

Fureur looked uneasy.

“But the Hotgo still live…”  

All eyes snap to Fureur.  

“*What?*”  

Fureur shrinks under their stares but continues at L'envie’s urging.  

“Someone told me a Hotgo woman still wanders the Steppe. She moves through places other tribes avoid—near Tao Khall, heading toward the Tail Mountains. Some kind of sanctuary? Orghana’s her name. Weird lady.”  

L'envie’s eyes widen.  

“Fureur, this might be the best news ever. To Tao Khaal, now! Timing doesn’t matter—we leave as fast as possible.”  

Fureur and Galare groan about walking again so soon, but L'envie doesn’t care. She has a lead. To Orghana—possibly her grandmother.

---

The walk takes a few hours. Rest is given when Galare whined about the weight of her armor. L'envie accept to rest as her red haired retainer has to suffer under the weight of red armor in a sunny plain. Watch the mountains growing and growing, L'envie has a knot in her stomach to reminder her this could be the place where she finds her only family, apart from her mother. L'envie eyes fix the water stream going out of the roc formation. She is so near of it and so far in the same time. She know it was time and nerves playing tricks with her. But it don't stop her to want go faster. To go further. Even if the travel has a negative outcome, she don't want to have regrets about family. It is after the second rest than L'envie walk in water. The end is in her grasp.

“There, L'envie !”

Neyaz point towards a plain door sculpted in the rocks.  The door was simple, without frills, without extravagance. Typical of the Steppe. Her heart was beating fast and L'envie couldn't take away her eyes from the door way.

“Thank you, everyone ! I will continue alone....”

“Are you sure, ma'am ?”

Galare's tone of voice sound worried. Her face show it too. Like Neyaz and Fureur faces. Only Claire is looking bored, like she is expecting L'envie behavior. And L'envie already know what she will do, if L'envie doesn't stop her now.

“Yes, I will go alone. Alone, alone. Without anyone, even you Claire.”

Now, she gets Claire attention. The xaela is glaring at her. The ninja was so used to act without L'envie orders than she is angry L'envie stops her to do her work. L'envie knows Claire only want her best but she has to do this leap of faith alone. This is for her, and only her. Her eyes glances to her retainers, her friends, going into the water stream entrance. They will await her there then. The raen go in front of the door and take a breath. It is now or never. Putting her two hands on the door and push. The door gives way to a dark place, with luminescent water and rock barriers. An oriental altar at the end, covered by a black roof. The place is clearly in ruins. The floor tiles are in pieces, the barriers are missing parts and the altar looks decrepit.The scent is filled the odor of moss, what L'envie find some parts on the barrieres. Plants grow in the gaps between the floor tiles. And L'envie wonders. What faith put this sanctuary here ? What made people leave and let it rot ? Her eyes scanned every detail in front of her before landing far, at the entrance of the altar. There, she found an old woman looking like she was praying. L'envie step went stronger. Time is not a friendly companion. The sound of her steps echoes in the sanctuary now as the woman finish her prayer before turning towards L'envie. [She is old. Very old. The wrinkles of her face marks the whims of time. Her hair is still dark blue with lighter parts.](https://characterhub.com/character/orghana-hotgo?v=1) Is this some way her real hair still going strong ? L'envie is curious. But first thing, first. She has to make sure.

“Hello. Sorry to bother you, but I search one Orghana ?”

“I may know her, child. What do you want to the old crone ..?”

Her voice is gravelly, as if she's not used to talking to anyone anymore.”

“Could you tell me where she is ?”

“I can tell her to speak with you. But why should she contact you ?”

L'envie was clearly waiting for the old woman to tell she was Orghana. But if the native of the Steppe wanted to play the unknown part, L'envie could play that game.

“Tell her.... Does she....”

L'envie stress is off the charts. Why is she so scared ? This woman looks not intimidating and her posture is not threatening at all. Maybe was she scared of the bond linking both ?

“Dethanon Hotgo let me a message for her !”

Her exclamation is told with strength. Strength she is scared to lose immediately. She doesn't know why she spoke about her father. Maybe to link them even more ? At least, she see the face of the woman lose colors. L'envie is sad she had to implicate her dad.

“This is better not to be joke, child !”

The face of the old crone went from stunned to angry fast. A quick reminder to L'envie the woman in front of her was a fierce xaela of the Steppe, ready to face perils for the honor and the survival of the tribe she outlived.

“Can you tell me where she is ?”
“In front of you ! Now tell ! What is this message of my son ?”

L'envie face turned into a sad smile. She was so sure. Now to confirm it, it is a simple joy to her soul.

“Her granddaughter is still alive, Désirée survived Dethanon.”

Orghana eyes begins to cry, her hands covering her face. Silent sobs echoes in the sanctuary, her legs gives out but L'envie is quick to support her. The raen knows she is not strong but she can help this gran of hers, even if she doesn't know this mink right now.

“I was so sure... My poor Dethanon...”, sobs Orghana.

L'envie cannot control herself and hug the old woman. She is everything she has now. The old crone cries her eyes out before the sobs slowly let place to silence. Her hands leave her face and Orghana face turns towards L'envie

“Do you... Do you know where is she ?”, she asks, afraid to hope. “My granddaughter... ?”

L'envie bites her lips, not knowing how to tell her. So she decides to let it go. For both of them.

“Right in front of you, gran...”

Orghana eyes go wide, extremely wide. Her eyes search L'envie face, she search the little details who would make sur L'envie is Désirée. Then, she sees L'envie eyes. The heterochromia. Orghana cries again, but this time, she hugs L'envie like her life depended on it. L'envie hugs her grandmother for dear life and cries too. She found a bit of her family. Both women cries for good five minuntes before each other at arm length, observing each other.

“Oh Désirée, you're beautiful ! You sure melted the heart of this poor Dethanon !”
“I cannot say but he was sure following me around when I was young...”
“If he could see you, he would go full rage on any people interested in you”

The last sentence was said with a smile. L'envie got back to her feet and she holds out her hand to her gran when a shout rang out.

“Don't touch her, bitch !”

L'envie didn't register what happened to her but she was ejected far from Orghana, a heavy hit in the jaw and her body slided on the floor of broken tiles. Thanking the Twelve she was still calm, she uses her white magic healing to recover and alleviate all her physical.  Taking attention to her surrounding, she put herself back on her feet before turning towards Orghana. There, she see a second person. [Svelte, short as her, wearing light steel armor per her top and bottom attire. Young. Same hair than Orghana but short, a bobcut, red face marks, heterochromia. Raen. Raen !? RAEN !!](https://characterhub.com/character/plaisir-daimer?v=1)

“How is this possible... ?”
“I dare you to approach her again ! I will put you down before you can even dare touch her again !”
“But finally, Fanny ! She doesn't want to harm me !”
“Nobody come here apart from us ! So she wants something from us, from you ! And I will not let her do something against.”
“But you see well how she is not xaela !”

The last sentence has some effect on the newcomer, Fanny, as she relent to stop glaring towards L'envie. The lost princess, for her part, gaze towards that impossibility. A second raen in Azim Steppe, one following the Hotgo traditions of face marks, but how could it be...

“Fanny, could you let Désirée approach, please ? For me ?”
“You play a dangerous game, old crone.”
“She brings me a sweet surprise. And to you too.”
“Ah, what surprise ?”

Fanny face, who turned towards her grandmother when she was talking to her, now turns towards L'envie. She is now curious. How strange how her emotions changed so easily. L'envie advanced towards the two remaining Hotgo. Face to face with Fanny, her mind finally register. But she cannot know if Fanny can understand.

“Fanny, this beautiful lady came here with a news I awaited for a long time. A news I longed for a long time I cannot tell today.”
“What news ?”
“Dethanon sent me letters, if you remember ?”

Fanny nodded to her gran information.

“In his letters, he explained what happened to him regularly, this is how he sent you to me. He continued to send letters after sending you, trying to explain why he chose to do that.”
“Didn't he explain he was trying to save me from death and so decided you were the best solution ?”
“That's true, in a way. As your mother didn't want you. She didn't need you.”

L'envie understood instantly. The line of succession. Her family was always on only child. A female. The future princess. Once that one born, no need to have another child part to create problems in the line. But it made no sense. All her books told her about spares, a second child to take care of the line if the first had problems. So why her mother sent...

“So what news she has ?”
“Désirée survived Dethanon”

L'envie repeated the sentence she told her grandmother. Fanny didn't seem to understand.

“Who is Désirée ?”
“Your sister.”

Orghana answered this time. Looking proud of the woman L'envie was. Without any information about her story, about how broken she was, how stupid she have been. The wrongs and the rights. Like she thought the best of her instantly. Maybe it was that, family love ? All the contrary of her mother ?

“A sister ? I have a sister.”
“Yes. A twin sister. Born two minutes before you.”
“And where she is ?”
“In front of you.”

The last answer from Orghana turned Fanny eyes wide as saucers, before whipping her eyes towards L'envie. Yes, twins. Désirée and Fanny. The two sides of the same coin. What the other could have been if their situation have been reversed. L'envie thought before what life would be in the Hotgo tribe. Fanny now could tell her. And heart caught up with her mind. A sister ! She had a sister ! 

L'envie throw herself to Fanny, taking her in her arms and crying again, even more than ever. She has a little sister. L'envie bury her face in Fanny shoulder. So much hurt, so much loneliness who could disappear with a sibling in her life ! Fanny didn't know how to react. Her arms stay a long time at her sides before slowly moving at L'envie sides. The other raen put her face in L'envie's crook of her neck. L'envie felt tears falling on her neck. Smiling, she strokes Fanny horns, as a sign of sister love. They're not alone anymore.

Turning her face towards Orghana, L'envie saw her smiling to them, with tears. happy tears, L'envie hopes. Their family is growing up. A reunion she wanted for a long turn into a bigger surprise. L'envie felt happy like never before. So happy.
